Today we will be taking about primate conservation.

The reasons we need to be worried about primate conservation are as follows:

  1. Primates serve as a key functions for the environment they inhabit.
  2. Primates provide a unique insight into human evolution.
  3. Primates are an umbrella species.This means that in order to stop their extinction the entire rain forest must also be conserved. Therefore their conservation benefits the entire ecosystem
  4. 10 percent of the worlds species live in the rain forest.
  5. Most importantly the rain forest produces a vast amount of oxygen for our planet.
